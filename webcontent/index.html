Brief Intro

PlayOn is an online quiz where football fanatics can test their knowledge in football. Participants get a chance to win exciting prizes every day. To participate in the quiz, login with your Facebook or Gmail account. To login click here.

How it works

A new question appears every hour of the day. This new question will be displayed once the user logs in to the website. A timer records the time taken by each user to answer the question. Participant answering most questions correctly for each day in minimum time is declared Winner for the Day and wins exciting prizes. Participants are given a window of 10 minutes to login and answer the question for the hour.

Grand Finale

The various Winners for the Day get a chance to participate in a Grand Finale. The winner of this finale event wins a Bumper prize and is declared Winner of the Tournament.
