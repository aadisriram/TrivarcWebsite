Brief Intro

TrivArc(Trivia Arcade) is an online quiz contest where the football fanatics can test their knowledge in football. Participants get a chance to win exciting prizes every day.

How it works

A new question appears every hour of the day(From 9am - 11pm IST). Participant answering the maximum questions correctly in a day in minimum time is declared Winner for the Day and wins exciting prizes. Participants must login using given option so that they can be contacted in case they win prizes.

Grand Finale

The various Winners for the Day get a chance to participate in a Grand Finale which will be conducted on the Final match day. The winner of this finale event wins a Bumper prize and is declared Winner of the Tournament.
